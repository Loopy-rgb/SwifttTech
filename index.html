A road cat's eye is a small reflective device embedded in the road surface to improve visibility. It helps drivers see lane markings, road edges, and curves, especially at night or in bad weather. The device consists of a durable housing with reflective elements that reflect vehicle headlights. Some versions include lights that flash or change color to signal road conditions.
